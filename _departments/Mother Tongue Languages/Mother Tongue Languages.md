<p><strong>Vision:</strong>
</p>
<p>Motivated Learners and proficient users of mother tongue languages who
appreciate and understand their respective culture.</p>
<p><strong>Mission:</strong>
</p>
<p>Provide a nurturing and thinking environment to develop studentsâ€™ love
for their own mother tongue language and culture.</p>
