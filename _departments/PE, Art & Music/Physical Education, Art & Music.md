<h2>Vision</h2>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop physically and aesthetically active students with a healthy
and resilient mind to overcome all challenges.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Mission</h2>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Mould the physical, social and aesthetic qualities of every student <em>through a rich and varied co-curricular programme. Develop students’ SE competencies </em>based
on the school’s core values.</p>
</li>
</ul>
