<h4><strong>Types of Membership</strong></h4>
<p>Ordinary Membership
<br>Ordinary Membership is open to all persons who are graduates of the school,
provided always that persons currently attending secondary schools, centralised
institutes or junior colleges shall not be admitted as members of the Society.
All Ordinary Members above 18 years of age shall have the rights to vote
and hold office in the society.</p>
<p>Life Membership
<br>Life Membership may be conferred on any Ordinary Member upon the payment
of a one-time subscription fee of $200. Life Members shall enjoy all the
rights and privileges of an Ordinary Member including the rights to vote
and hold office in the Society. Life Members shall not be required to pay
any annual subscription.</p>
<p>Honorary Membership
<br>Honorary Membership may be conferred by the Society on such persons distinguished
by service to the school as the Committee may think fit, either for life
or for such period as the Committee may in any case consider appropriate.
Honorary Members shall have no rights to vote and hold office in the Society.</p>
<p>Associate Membership
<br>Associate Membership may be conferred by the Society, upon application,
on school staff, parents of all former students (alumni) and on such persons
who in the opinion of the Committee are related to the school and/or would
contribute to the futherance of the objectives of the Society. Associate
Members shall have no rights to vote and hold office in the Society.</p>
<p>Patrons
<br>The Committee may appoint, from time to time, distinguished persons in
public life to be Patrons of the Society. Patrons shall have no rights
to vote and hold office in the Society.</p>
<p>Advisors
<br>The Committee may appoint, from time to time, desirable persons to serve
as Advisors to the society. Advisors shall have no rights to vote and hold
office in the Society.</p>
<h4><strong>Application For Membership</strong></h4>
<p>Ex-students who wish to join the Society as a Life Member should contact
the school at bpps@moe.edu.sg for the application form.</p>
