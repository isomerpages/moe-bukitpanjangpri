<p>In order to forge better school ties and community development between
the school and former students, ten former students came forward to form
the BPPS Alumni Association. The Association was officially recognised
by the Registry of Societies on 28&nbsp;May 1998.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Objectives of Alumni</strong>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Based on the constitution, the objectives of the alumni are:</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To forge school ties and community
involvement between Bukit Panjang Primary School and its former students</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To provide members with the opportunities
of helping to develop the school for the &nbsp;benefit of their children</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To encourage volunteerism among
members</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; For members, as a fraternity, to
offer, provide and contribute expertise and resources</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
