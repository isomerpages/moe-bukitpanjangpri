<h2>School Rules</h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to wear the prescribed school uniform and modification to
the uniform is not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All students must be in full uniform/school Physical Education (PE) attire
when they are in school or when attending any activities organised by the
school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All students must be in school uniform during school days except on days
when they have PE, CCA and PAL.</p>
</li>
</ol>

<h2>General discipline</h2>
<h5>Attendance</h5>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students must attend school daily on school days. Skipping lessons and
truancy are serious offenses.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h5>Conduct</h5>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Students who are Singapore citizens must sing the National Anthem and
take the Pledge. Students will take the Pledge with the right fist over
the heart.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Orderliness and silence must be observed during flag-raising ceremony.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students may only leave the classroom with permission of the teacher.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Eating and drinking is allowed in designated places.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students must queue when buying food or drinks from the stalls in the
canteen. They must returned used utensils to the respective receptacles
provided in the canteen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students must help to keep the school clean by not littering.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students should keep the toilets clean and flush after use.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All students must vacate the classrooms during recess. Students are allowed
into the classrooms only when there is a teacher with them.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are reminded to walk in an orderly manner from place to place.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to adhere to instructions pertaining to the use of facilities
posted at different areas of the school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students should be respectful, honest and courteous to everyone.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to be cooperative and respect prefects at all times.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are strongly discouraged from bringing mobile phones/smart watches
to school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Toys, handheld games consoles and other electronic devices not related
to any school activities are not allowed to be brought to school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to be responsible for their own belongings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are not allowed to have any weapons in their possession. They
are also not allowed to bring any weapon-like items which can be used or
intended to be used to cause harm to others.</p>
</li>
</ol>
