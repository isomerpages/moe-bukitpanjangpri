<p><strong>Purpose of the programme</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide opportunities for students to display the school’s values of&nbsp;<strong>Respect, Responsibility, Compassion, Integrity, Resilience and Gratitude</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop students’ 21st Century Competencies and leadership skills through
OAE programmes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To equip students with outdoor skills, knowledge and values and apply
them in authentic experiences.</p>
</li>
</ol>
